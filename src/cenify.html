Cenify is a smart home platform that stands to be universal across smart devices. No hub, all from one app.

UI/UX Designer

Rochester, NY Winter 2015 - Present

    Creating a smart home interface and experience that’s easier then turning on a light bulb is no easy task.


    The interfaces and user flows I designed are basely solely around simplicity and frictionless actions. It is imperative in this new realm of smart homes that the product provides convenience, not another unnecessary process.


    Taking use cases from a traditional home environment and translating into a smart solution is one of the most interesting parts of my job.



    Cenify also works with other companies providing software applications to up and coming smart technology. Recently we’ve been collaborating with a company developing a smart heated jacket. I developed interfaces for both mobile and wearable, giving the user the ability to control the temperature of their jacket from devices like the Apple Watch.
